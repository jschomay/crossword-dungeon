  1. Dragon treasure unlock message interrupts flow — DRAGON_TREASURE_DEF.onEvent
  calls ctx.showInteraction() and ctx.render() when dragon is defeated (line 425).
  This pops up an interaction mid-room-resolution, before the player has dismissed
  the combat result. This could be jarring or cause double-popup issues.
  2. Simm marks hasMetSimm = true immediately on level:start — so even if the player
  never finds Simm on a level, they'll get "repeat" greetings next time. The dialogue
   is built at level:start before the player meets Simm, so it may not reflect the
  actual HP/mana the player has when they finally talk.
  3. dismissPopup marks extra rooms as completed (line 921-924) for non-shop/non-boss
   rooms, but markExtraRoomSolved is also on the RunContext — there may be
  double-completion paths.
  4. Treasure Hunter handleInput doesn't mark the room solved after talking (space
  just returns true). The room stays visible and talkable forever. (Same for Simm —
  it disappears via completed in dismissPopup, but only if the player dismisses while
   still in the room.)
  5. pickAdjacentEmptyCell uses Math.floor(Math.random() * candidates.length) rather
  than ROT.RNG — so it's not seeded/deterministic like the rest of generation. Minor,
   but consistent with the rest.
